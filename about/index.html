who is retna studios?

Hey!

thanks for stopping by and checking us out, we hope you enjoy your stay.

we are a game development studio based out of Dunedin, New Zealand. The games we like to develop are small bite size co-op games that you can sit down and enjoy with a friend after work.

our company values boldness, progression, collaboration and fun. This comes in no particular order. bold decision making makes things interesting, always striving to progress on the daily, spend life collaborating with others and try your best to enjoy life.
