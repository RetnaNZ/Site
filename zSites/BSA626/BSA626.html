The Beginning

PROJECT:

For this project I will build a high quality intro cinematic for a game called "bashful". Simple, Clean and focused on the character and what he does.


The mechanics of the game is to "push the characters buttons" via clicking the character enough to make him/her/it so annoyed that he/she/it will burst out in anger to start destroying everything and anything in its way.


This game idea was inspired by the music video "The Verve - Bitter Sweet Symphony". and looking at the Elmo puppet in the classroom.
